Guide

The Goal

The objective of this Micro-PK (Psychokinesis) trainer is to use your mental intention to influence the movement of randomly generated particles. Your goal is to "will" them to conform to the shape you have selected.

Two Ways to Analyze

  • 1. The Average (Static PK): The standard Z-Score. This measures your ability to keep the bubbles organized for the entire duration.
  • 2. The Trend (Dynamic PK): This measures the slope of your session. If you start chaotic but "tame" the bubbles over time (the line goes down), you will see an "Improving" trend. This indicates you are actively reducing entropy.

Interpreting Z-Scores

The Z-Score measures how far your result is from pure chance.

  • Z ≈ 0.00: Pure Chance.
  • Z > 1.65: Significant (1 in 20 odds).
  • Z > 2.33: Highly Significant (1 in 100 odds).
